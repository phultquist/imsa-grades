About

This site was made for students, parents, teachers and all other community members to have clean and consistent access to grades at the Illinois Mathematics and Science Academy. 2012-2017 Data was aquired through a 2017 FOIA Request by a Class of 2018 student. Data from 2016-2020 was gathered from the Office of Institutional Research, but only 2018-2020 data was used.

Note this is not an official site. If you find an error, please report it.
